Welcome to My Hobby Website

League of Legends (LoL) is a popular online multiplayer battle arena video game developed and published by Riot Games. Launched in 2009, it has become one of the most played and watched esports games worldwide. In LoL, players assume the roles of "champions," each with unique abilities and playstyles, and participate in teams of five to compete against one another.

I started playing League of Legends in Season 5 (2015) and fell in love with the strategic gameplay, and the ever-expanding roster of champions to master. This hobby website is dedicated to sharing my passion for LoL and connecting with fellow players and enthusiasts.
